Owls

In this website, you will learn all about owls! Owls are nocturnal birds of prey. They have heightened senses, such as binocular vision and binaural hearing, which allow them to more easily catch their prey, which often includes weasels, bats and smaller birds. Owls can also rotate their heads and necks 270Â°, due to their 14 neck vertebrae. Owls are very interesting creatures, varying in type and representing different things all over the world.





Interesting facts about owls:

  • Owls don't have eyeballs
  • A group of owls is refered to as a "parliament".
  • Owls are not capable of chewing their food; they swallow it whole.
